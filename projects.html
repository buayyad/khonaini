Projects

Initiatives in civic engagement, research, and public accountability.

Raqib50

Co-founded 2011

A parliament monitoring platform established to follow up the actions of members of parliament in Kuwait. Raqib50 promotes transparency and public accountability by tracking parliamentary activities, voting records, and legislative processes, making this information accessible to Kuwaiti citizens.

The project received the Kuwait Award for Excellence and Creativity in Digital Media (2021) from the Ministry of Youth Affairs, and the Franco-German Prize for Human Rights and the Rule of Law (2017) from the Franco-German Council of Ministers — the first such recognition for a project from the Gulf region.

Siyaaq

Co-founded 2021

A research and consultancy organization driven by social missions. Siyaaq works at the intersection of research, policy, and social impact, providing evidence-based analysis and strategic consultancy to support civic and social development initiatives.

Civic Activism & Reform

2005 – Present

Since his undergraduate years at Kuwait University, Abdullah has been actively involved in civic movements including campaigns for women's suffrage and electoral reform in Kuwait. His ongoing work focuses on freedom of expression, constitutional rights, and governmental transparency.
