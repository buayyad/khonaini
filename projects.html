Aswatna: Educating for Inclusion

En.v with Data4Change and CTLR at Gulf University for Science and Technology

Co-designed and facilitated two rounds of ToT for Aswatna: a participatory bilingual program to empower students and teachers to create social change within their schools by using Participatory Action Research methods (2021-2024).

Enbat: Nurturing Transformational Leadership

En.v with BluHat, commissioned by KIPCO as flagship CSR training

Co-designed and facilitated a ToT for Enbat: an 8 weeks training program for freshly university graduates to cultivate soft skills for work and for life. The program has four modules on: Self, Systems, and Critical Thinking; Cross Cultural Communication; Design Thinking; and Work Culture & Applied Skills (2023-2024).
